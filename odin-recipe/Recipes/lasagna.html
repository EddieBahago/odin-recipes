Lasagna

The perfect lasagna recipe made with parmesan ricotta cheese filling, melted mozzarella cheese

Such an amazing meal to end the day

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 small Onion finely diced
  • 1 lb. Lean Ground Beef
  • 3/4 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon Salt
